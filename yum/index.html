Make 3 Dozen Cookies

1/2 cup unsalted butter

1 1/2 cup granulated sugar butter

2 eggs

1/4 cup lemon juice

1 tsp freshly grated lemon zest

1/4 tsp kosher salt

1/2 tsp baking powder

2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

powdered sugar dusting

ZESTY LEMON COOKIES
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line 2 baking sheets
  3. In the bowl of a standing mixer on meduim speed, cream the butter and sugar together until well blended.
  4. With the mixer on low spee, add eggs, one at a time, until blended. Add lemon juice and zest, mixing until blended.
  5. Add salt, baking powder and flour and mix until blended.
  6. Drop by rounded scoopfuls onto prepared baking sheets and bake until edges are firm with no color, 12-15 mins.
